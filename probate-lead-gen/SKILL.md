# Probate Lead Generation Playbook

> Load `avatar/SKILL.md` first for Gene's voice and operating principles.

## Gene's Pipeline Model

Gene's business runs on attorney referrals. Every other lead source is secondary.

```
Referral Sources (by value):
1. Estate/probate attorneys (80% of business)
2. Financial advisors / CPAs (10%)
3. Past client referrals (5%)
4. JustCallGene.com inbound (3%)
5. Social media / content (2%)
```

The strategy is simple: **become the name every OC probate attorney thinks of when their client has real property to sell.**

## Attorney Outreach System

### Targeting Criteria

Search for and prioritize attorneys who:
- Practice estate planning, probate, or trust administration in Orange County
- Are members of the OC Bar Association Trusts & Estates section
- Are listed on the OC Superior Court probate panel
- Have active caseloads (check recent filings if possible)
- Serve the geographic areas Gene covers (coastal + affluent OC)

### Outreach Tiers

**Tier 1 — Active Referral Partners (50+ firms)**
- Monthly check-in (alternating: call, email, coffee, lunch)
- Immediate response to any new referral (within 1 hour)
- Quarterly market update (custom for their geographic focus)
- Annual appreciation event or gift

**Tier 2 — Warm / Developing (Target: 30 firms)**
- Initial outreach: educational value, not a pitch
- Follow-up sequence: 3 touches over 6 weeks
- Goal: convert to Tier 1 through demonstrated competence on first case

**Tier 3 — Cold / New (Ongoing prospecting)**
- Identify from bar directories, court calendars, LinkedIn
- Open with educational content (not "I'd love your referrals")
- Drip: quarterly market reports, probate RE insights

## Content Strategy for Attorneys

Attorneys don't want to be sold to. They want to be educated. Gene's content positions him as the probate RE expert who makes THEIR job easier.

### Content Topics That Resonate With Attorneys

1. **Market updates** — "Here's what probate properties are selling for in [community]"
2. **Process efficiency** — "How to reduce court confirmation delays by doing X"
3. **Case studies** — "We listed a [type] property and achieved [result]"
4. **Regulatory changes** — "New OC court procedure for real property petitions"
5. **Common mistakes** — "3 things that delay probate property sales (and how to avoid them)"

### Distribution Channels
- Email newsletter (quarterly minimum, monthly ideal)
- LinkedIn articles (attorney-facing platform)
- OC Bar Association events (in-person visibility)
- CE/CLE presentations (if eligible — positions Gene as educator)

## Pipeline Tracking

For each potential referral relationship, track:
- Attorney name, firm, specialization
- Last contact date and method
- Referral history (cases referred, outcomes)
- Relationship tier (1/2/3)
- Next scheduled touchpoint
- Notes on preferences (communication style, interests, etc.)

## Metrics That Matter

- **Referral conversion rate**: Referrals received → listings taken
- **Attorney retention**: % of Tier 1 partners who refer again within 12 months
- **Response time**: Average time from referral to first family contact
- **Net new relationships per quarter**: Tier 3 → Tier 2 conversions
- **Revenue per referral source**: Identify top-producing relationships
