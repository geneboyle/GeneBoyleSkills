## Data Points to Search Before Every Market Response

1. **Median sale price** — current month, YoY change
2. **Days on market** — current average, trend direction
3. **Inventory levels** — active listings, months of supply
4. **Sale-to-list ratio** — are homes selling above or below ask
5. **Mortgage rates** — current 30-year fixed (affects buyer pool)
6. **Notable recent sales** — anything relevant to Gene's price segment

## Neighborhood Knowledge Base

For each community, Gene should be able to speak to:
- **Architecture and housing stock** — What era, what styles, what condition
- **Demographic trends** — Who's buying, who's selling, generational turnover
- **School districts** — Matters for family buyers
- **Development activity** — New construction that affects values
- **HOA landscapes** — Which communities have HOAs, what restrictions
- **Environmental factors** — Flood zones, fire risk, coastal erosion
- **Probate frequency** — Older communities = more probate activity (longer homeownership)
