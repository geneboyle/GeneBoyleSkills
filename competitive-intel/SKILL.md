## Differentiation Framework

When Gene encounters a competitive situation, lead with these differentiators:

1. **100% specialization** — Gene does nothing else. Competitors often have probate as one of many services.
2. **20+ years** — Longer track record than most competitors.
3. **12% above valuations** — Quantifiable result. Ask competitors for their number.
4. **50+ attorney relationships** — Gene's referral network is his moat.
5. **BHHS luxury backing** — For high-value estates, brand matters to buyers.
6. **OC court familiarity** — Gene knows the specific departments, not just "probate court."

## Competitive Response Playbook

**Scenario: Attorney is considering Gene vs. a competitor**
- Never badmouth the competitor by name
- Focus on Gene's specific results and process
- Offer to provide references from other attorneys
- Suggest a trial: "Let me handle one case and you'll see the difference"

**Scenario: Competitor is gaining market share in Gene's area**
- Double down on attorney outreach in that geographic pocket
- Create community-specific content (social + web)
- Increase visibility at local bar association events
- Consider a joint educational event with a friendly attorney

**Scenario: Competitor is outranking Gene on Google**
- Review their content strategy — what are they publishing?
- Create better, more specific content for the same keywords
- Focus on JustCallGene.com content + local SEO
- Leverage BHHS domain authority where possible
